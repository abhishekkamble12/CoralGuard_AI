### Escalation Protocol
1. **First Response (within 2 hours)**: Contact the regional marine park authority for the affected jurisdiction.
2. **Secondary Escalation (within 24 hours)**: If the event spans multiple jurisdictions or is of exceptional severity, escalate to NOAA Coral Reef Watch and the national environmental ministry.
3. **International Notification (within 72 hours)**: For events affecting World Heritage Sites or transboundary reefs, notify the International Coral Reef Initiative (ICRI) secretariat at icri@icriforum.org.

### Report Format for Authority Notification
When contacting authorities, include:
- Subject line: "[CORALGUARD ALERT] [Risk Level] - [Location] - [Date]"
- Body must contain:
  - GPS coordinates of affected area
  - Estimated area affected (hectares)
  - AI classification result and confidence score
  - Environmental readings (SSTA, TSA, depth)
  - Number and type of species affected (if known)
  - Photographs attached
  - Recommended immediate action from CoralGuard AI
